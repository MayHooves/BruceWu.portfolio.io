## Minecraft Modding Project: Magical Land

This project is an ongoing Minecraft mod that integrates characters from My Little Pony into the game. I took this as a challenge to master a new creative suite (Blockbench) from scratch.

I am responsible for the entire asset production pipeline, including 3D modeling, rigging, and animation. To achieve a more fluid and expressive look, I extensively applied "Squash and Stretch" principles, giving the characters a lively, "bouncy" feel that transcends the standard blocky aesthetic. This project demonstrates my ability to quickly adapt to unfamiliar software and execute a complete technical workflow.

GitHub Repository: https://github.com/Magical-Land-Official/Magical-Land
