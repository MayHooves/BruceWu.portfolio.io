## What I've learned

This is a practice clip created during class, where I focused on mastering the **Blocking** phase using **Stepped Mode**. This exercise taught me the critical importance of solidifying key poses before moving to spline.

For the backflip, I iteratively refined the **animation curves** for every part of the body to ensure a natural and fluid motion. Additionally, I added a reactive bounce to the block beneath the character upon takeoff to enhance the sense of weight and dynamic impact.
